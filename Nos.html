My Big No's

 

Let's start with what I won’t do. If you want me to draw something, and it falls under these categories, I will simply decline the offer:

  1. Art that promotes hate speech or intolerance against marginalized groups (feels like this should be a given but you never know)
  2. Art that is more than mildly suggestive in nature, as I find it more difficult to do correctly and it makes me personally uncomfortable (what reads as suggestive and not suggestive is at my discretion).
  3. Stuff with outright inappropriate sexual themes is out of the question. Things like cartoonish gore and violence are at my discretion.
  4. Overcomplicated designs like complex mechanisms or fabrics with small, intricate patterns or folds (this one is more subjective than a definite no like the previous three, be sure to ask).
  5. If you ask me to draw you a sheet of hands or feet in various poses I will find you. Or horses. Horses are ridiculous. Go look at a picture of a horse and tell me that they're not. You can’t.

...Ok, that last one was more of a joke. I don’t actually have that many restrictions on what I will accept. Of course, if I feel a project is too wide a scope for me to do in a timely manner, I will advise you accordingly. All I ask is that you are patient with me.
